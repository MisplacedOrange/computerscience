The Georgian region is one of the most beautiful vacation destinations in Central Ontario.
Crystal lakes and miles of unspoiled shoreline await vacationers from around the world. And it's waiting for you.


Georgian Bay Boats and Cottages, located on Clearwater Bay near Parry Sound, offers the best of family-size cottages and recreational facilities.

Some of our amenities include:

  • Fully equipped rental cottages
  • Boat rentals
  • Guided bird-watching tours

Feel free to explore our beautiful image gallery to discover the perfect cottage for your family. We can't wait to welcome you!
