Georgian Bay Boats and Cottages

The Georgian Bay region is one of the most beautiful vacation destinations in Central Ontario.
Crystal lakes and miles of unspoiled shoreline await vacationiers from around the world. And it's waiting for you.

Georgian Bay Boats and Cottages, located on Cleanwater Bay near Parry Sound, offers the best of family-size cottages and recreational facilities.

Some of our amenities include:

  • Fully equipped rental cottages
  • Boat rentals
  • Guided bird watching

Please browse through our gallery of images to find the cottage that's just right for your family. We look forward to your visit!
