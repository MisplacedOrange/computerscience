Home Cottages Boat Rentals Things to do About Us

These cottages are the most rustic that we have. They are fully equipped and are ready for immediate occupancy this summer. All of these cottages are located on the shores of a most beautiful lake and all have their own canoes and motor boats.

Set in the woods these cottages afford you an opportunity to experience the thrill of watching wildlife in its most natural habitat. You will get the chance to experience bears, moose and beaver as well as a variety of birds.
